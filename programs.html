Programs

Programs for speaking, debate, leadership, and soft skills.

Junior Club

Senior Level

Grades 6-10

A more advanced track for middle and high school students who need stronger speaking structure, debate confidence, leadership presence, and real-world communication readiness.

  • Prepared speeches with clear openings, evidence, transitions, and conclusions
  • Impromptu speaking for interviews, class discussions, and spontaneous questions
  • Debate, persuasive speaking, rebuttal, and respectful disagreement
  • Town hall leadership roles, facilitation, teamwork, and peer feedback
  • Progress goals connected to band levels, competitions, and certificates

Weekly rhythm

Every program follows a practical learning loop.

Warm up, practice, receive feedback, reflect, and try again. That repetition is where confidence grows.

  1. 01

    Warm up

    Low-pressure activities get students speaking early.

  2. 02

    Build

    Students learn a speaking, leadership, or teamwork skill.

  3. 03

    Perform

    Every student practices in front of others.

  4. 04

    Improve

    Feedback turns each session into progress.
