Band Level System

A visible progression path for speaking, leadership, and confidence.

Students advance through color bands as they demonstrate stronger communication habits, leadership responsibility, creativity, teamwork, and presentation confidence.

White

Getting started with voice, listening, and participation.

Yellow

Building comfort with short speaking activities and eye contact.

Orange

Using simple speech structure and clearer delivery.

Green

Adding teamwork, feedback, and confident participation.

Pink

Growing creativity, storytelling, and expressive speaking.

Red

Practicing persuasive speaking and stronger audience connection.

Brown

Taking leadership roles and supporting peers.

Black

Showing advanced preparation, presence, and responsibility.

Purple

Demonstrating high-level communication, debate, and facilitation.

Blue

Representing mastery, mentorship, and future-ready leadership.

Achievement tracking

Progress should feel earned, visible, and motivating.

The band system gives students a clear reason to keep practicing. Each level can connect to speaking tasks, leadership activities, reflection, competitions, and certificates in the future member portal.
